Our Solutions
Eco Power Solutions is committed to leading India’s transition away from fossil fuels and meeting the rising demand for energy in a sustainable manner by
delivering cleaner and smarter energy choices; thereby reducing India’s carbon footprint.



Wind

EcoPower Solutions is a leading wind power developer with a proven track record and a portfolio of 4.27 GW commissioned and committed utility-scale solar projects across India. Having one of the largest portfolios of wind energy projects in the country, it is spread across seven states of Maharashtra,Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh



Solar

Eco Power's Solar has a portfolio of more than 7GW of ground-mount utility-scale, over 750MW of rooftop and distributed generation projects across the country & installed over 55000 Solar Water Pumps in India till date. It also offers a diverse line of solar solutions for both urban and rural markets – these include rooftop solutions, solar pumps, and power packs among others. Tata Power Solar is committed to enabling solar everywhere and aims to provide energy access to millions of people across the country via its integrated solar solutions.



Hydro Power

We at Eco Power Solutions are a leading supplier of electromechanical systems and services (“from water-to-wire“) for hydropower plants and one of the leading in the world market for hydraulic power generation.The Company has an installed hydro capacity of 880 MW for the domestic market. Pioneering the concept of cheap, clean and pollution free energy, our hydro stations are used to primarily supply power for the associated industrial areas.We in exclusive partnership with AquaTurbine Tech develop hydro power projects in India and Nepal.



Geo Thermal

Eco Power solutions has recently from the year 2020 started its research in the field of geo thermal energy. Geothermal is the power of the earth beneath our feet and can be used as an energy source to generate electricity, heat and cool homes, and so much more. We in collaboration with Geothermal rising have developed technologies to advance the use of geothermal energy as a clean, renewable and domestic power source.
