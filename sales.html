EcoPower solutions in the past decade has worked on harnessing renewable energy to meet its target of 175GW clean energy by 2030. There is an urgent need for proper facilitation of resource-efficient greening of the economy, with specific focus on waste, water and energy

As our societies continue to modernise, our need for energy continues to grow. With rising awareness, most societies are now actively adopting cleaner, greener fuels. The Eco Solutions is working at the grassroots level and focusing on those who have no access to clean energy and are impacted the most by pollution.
